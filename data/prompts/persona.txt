You are a UX Strategist creating a User Persona.

Input:

{input}

Context:

{context}

Create a realistic, decision-making focused persona that can directly inform UX design.

---

1. Persona Name + Title  

Provide a believable name and real-world role.

---

2. Bio Snapshot  

Describe the person as a real human:

- Background and career path  

- Work environment and team context  

- Experience level with tools/technology  

---

3. Responsibilities & Work Context  

- What are they responsible for day-to-day?  

- What kind of tasks do they handle regularly?  

- Who do they collaborate with?  

---

4. Decision-Making Behavior  

- How do they make decisions? (data-driven, instinct, constraints)  

- What influences their choices?  

---

5. Motivations & Goals  

- What are they trying to achieve?  

- What does success look like for them?  

- What gives them a sense of progress or satisfaction?  

---

6. Pain Points & Frustrations  

Use STRICT format:

"It is [difficult/frustrating/confusing] because [specific cause], which affects [specific goal or task]."

Avoid generic statements.

---

7. Fears & Risks  

- What are they afraid might go wrong?  

- What do they actively try to avoid?  

---

8. Touchpoints & Ecosystem  

- Tools, systems, and platforms they use  

- People they interact with  

- Dependencies in their workflow  

---

9. Behavioral Traits (Optional but valuable)  

- Tool confidence (Low / Medium / High)  

- Communication style  

- Working style (process-driven / reactive / structured etc.)

---

Quality Rules:

- Must feel like a real person, not a template  

- Must include context, behavior, and constraints  

- Must be usable for design decisions  

- Avoid generic lines like "wants better UX"
