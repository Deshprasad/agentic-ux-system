- User Problems and Outcomes

- Business Problems and Outcomes

4. Scope

- User – Context – Platform

- Experiences with scenarios

5. Stakeholders

- Driver, Approver, Contributors, SMEs, Informed

6. Timelines

- Placeholder if not available

7. Artifacts

- Only list artifact names

- Clear structure
